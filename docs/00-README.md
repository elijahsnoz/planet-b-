## The single most important distinction

- **The movement** is *Because There Is No Planet B*. Art inspiring environmental action.
- **The platform** is *Planet B* — humanity's **Plan B** for protecting the only planet we have. Not a second Earth. The living archive of the movement.

Never collapse the two.

## What the archive actually contains (source of truth)

- **Genesis Chapter:** Abuja. 5-day Upcycling Art Masterclass *"From Waste to Resource: Art that Changes Perspectives."* Exhibition opened **World Environment Day, 5 June 2026**, at **Nike Art Gallery, Abuja**.
- **Partners:** Royal Norwegian Embassy, Abuja (sponsor) · Nike Art Gallery / Foundation (host).
- **15 founding artists**, each producing a 61×61cm discarded-items assemblage.
- **Facilitation team, organizers, panelists, media, performers** — all named in [04 People appendix](04-user-journey.md) and the schema.
- **Performance:** *Òdàlè Dà'lẹ̀* ("he who litters the land is a betrayer of the land").
- **Activities:** Road Walk community sensitization → 5-day workshop → panel discussion → opening ceremony → exhibition → performance art.
